Support guide

Frequently asked questions

Platform & capabilities

Are the environments real or simulated?

Labs run real tools, real cloud accounts, and real compute — learners practice on the same environments they'll use on the job. For scenarios that are impractical to provision — clinical encounters, role play, complex multi-tenant configurations — you can deliver AI-powered simulations through the same governed platform.

How are cloud costs handled for large programs?

Budgets, quotas, idle timers, and auto-teardown cap spend per learner and per cohort, with usage analytics for visibility.

Shared infrastructure or isolated environments?

Each learner gets an isolated, resettable environment governed by your policies.

AI & agentic capabilities

How does the AI Gateway work?

A governed layer that routes learner access to approved AI models under your budget and policy controls.

Do you support agentic workflows and copilots?

Yes — labs can include agentic workflows, copilots, and in-lab AI assistance.

Can we deliver apps or simulations we've built ourselves?

Yes. Build with Claude Code or any tool you like, bring the app to Vocareum, and we deliver it to your learners at scale — authentication, resource management, and governed AI access included.

How does the AI Tutor prevent hallucinations and cheating?

The tutor is grounded in lab context and assessment-aware, scoping help to hints and explanations rather than answers.

Integrations & ecosystem

Which LMS platforms do you integrate with?

Any LTI-compliant LMS, with gradebook passback and class dashboards.

Do you support SSO and embedded delivery?

Yes — SSO and APIs for embedding labs into your own course and platform experience.

Which clouds and data platforms are supported?

AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, on-prem, and federal/research compute.

Assessment & validation

Can you auto-grade code and cloud infrastructure tasks?

Yes — code, notebooks, cloud configurations, and deployments can be auto-graded.

Are you compliant with enterprise security and privacy standards?

Vocareum supports enterprise security and education privacy requirements; contact sales for specifics.
